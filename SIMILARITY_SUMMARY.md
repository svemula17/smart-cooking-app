## Price Positioning Options

### Freemium (Like Mealime, Kitchendary)
- **Free:** Recipe browse, basic meal planning, shopping list
- **Premium ($2.99-$4.99/mo):** Macro matching, AI substitutions, grocery delivery integration
- **Pros:** Lowest barrier to entry, largest addressable market
- **Cons:** Lower ARPU

### Premium Subscription (Like Ollie, Melio)
- **Price:** $9.99-$19.99/month
- **Includes:** Everything (planning, macros, AI, delivery)
- **Pros:** Higher lifetime value, focused user base
- **Cons:** Smaller addressable market

### Hybrid (Like Paprika)
- **One-time purchase:** $4.99-$8.99
- **Optional subscription:** Premium features or cloud sync
- **Pros:** Captures both segments
- **Cons:** Confusing pricing
