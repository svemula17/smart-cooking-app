## Common questions

### Can't log in?

- Double-check the email is spelled exactly the way you registered (no spaces, correct case for the domain).
- Your password must be at least 8 characters.
- If you've forgotten your password, **email us** — automatic password reset is on the roadmap but isn't live yet.

### App won't load recipes / shows "network error"

- Check that you have a working internet connection. The app shows a red banner at the top when you're offline.
- If you're online but still see errors, force-close the app and reopen it.
- If the problem persists, email us with the steps you took — there might be a temporary backend issue we're already on.

### How do I delete my account?

In the app: **Profile → Delete account**. You'll be asked to confirm twice (the second prompt is the irreversible one).

All your data — saved recipes, meal plans, pantry, shopping lists, nutrition logs, household memberships — is permanently removed from our database within 30 days. Backup copies are purged within 90 days. If you change your mind after deletion, you'd need to register fresh.

### How is my data used?

We don't sell it, don't share it with advertisers, and don't track you across other apps. Full details in our [Privacy Policy](./privacy).

### Does the app work offline?

Partial. You can browse recipes you've already viewed (cached) and view your saved meal plans, but logging meals, syncing pantry changes, and AI Chef all require a network connection. The app shows a banner when you're offline.

### Is calorie/macro data accurate?

The numbers shown are best-effort estimates based on standard ingredient weights. They're not lab-verified. **Don't rely on them for medical, allergen, or dietary-restriction decisions** — consult a qualified nutritionist for that.

## Reporting a bug

When you email us about a bug, please include:

1. **What you were trying to do** — e.g., "open the AI chat tab"
2. **What happened instead** — e.g., "app froze for 10 seconds then closed itself"
3. **Your phone model + OS version** — Settings → About → System version
4. **App version** — open the Profile tab, scroll all the way to the bottom

Screenshots and screen recordings help a lot.
