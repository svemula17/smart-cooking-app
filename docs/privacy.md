# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 17, 2026
**Last updated:** May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the SmartCooking mobile application (the "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service.

If you have questions, contact us at **saikumarvemula.us@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly
When you create an account or use the App, we collect:

- **Account information**: name, email address, password (stored only as a one-way bcrypt hash)
- **Profile preferences**: daily calorie / macro goals, dietary restrictions, favorite cuisines
- **Recipe interactions**: recipes you view, save, rate, or cook
- **Meal plans**: scheduled meals, dates, and meal types
- **Pantry inventory**: ingredient names, quantities, units, and expiry dates you log
- **Shopping lists**: items, quantities, and check status
- **Nutrition logs**: meals consumed, servings, and calculated macros
- **Household data** (optional): household name, invited members, cooking schedules, chore assignments, and shared expenses

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device information**: device type, OS version, app version
- **Usage data**: features used, session length, errors encountered
- **Crash reports**: stack traces and device state at the time of a crash (if you opt in to crash reporting)

### 1.3 Information from third parties
We do not currently buy or import personal data from third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Authenticate you and keep you signed in
- Save your recipes, meal plans, pantry, shopping lists, and nutrition history
- Personalize recipe recommendations based on your preferences
- Calculate daily nutrition summaries
- Coordinate household features (cook rotation, chores, expenses) with other members of your household
- Send password reset emails (if you request one)
- Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** use your data for advertising, sell it to brokers, or share it with marketers.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only with:

### 3.1 Service providers (sub-processors)
- **Supabase, Inc.** — managed PostgreSQL database hosting (US-based). Stores your account, recipes, meal plans, and other content.
- **Railway Corp.** — backend service hosting (US-based). Runs the API services that process your requests.
- **Sentry, Inc.** — crash and error reporting (US-based). Receives anonymized stack traces if you opt in.
- **Apple, Inc.** and **Google LLC** — for push notifications and app store distribution.

We require these providers to handle your data in line with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

### 3.2 Other household members
If you join a household within the App, other members of that household can see:
- Your display name
- Meals you have scheduled or cooked within the household
- Chore assignments
- Shared expenses you have paid or owe

They cannot see your pantry, private meal plans, or nutrition logs.

### 3.3 Legal disclosures
We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of users, ourselves, or the public.

### 3.4 Business transfers
If we are acquired or merged, your information may be transferred. We will notify you and give you a chance to delete your account before the transfer takes effect.

---

## 4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information through the App's profile screen
- **Delete** your account and all associated data — available in-app under Profile → Delete Account, or by emailing us
- **Export** your data — email us and we will provide your data in JSON format within 30 days
- **Opt out** of crash reporting — toggle in the App's settings

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR / CCPA, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

- Your account record and all associated content (recipes saved, meal plans, pantry, shopping lists, nutrition logs, household memberships) are deleted within 30 days
- Backups are purged within 90 days
- Anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely

---

## 6. Security

We protect your information with:

- HTTPS / TLS for all network traffic
- bcrypt password hashing (cost factor 12)
- Short-lived JWT access tokens (15 minutes) with refresh token rotation
- Database access restricted to our backend services
- Encrypted database connections to Supabase

No system is perfectly secure. If we learn of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

SmartCooking is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 8. International Users

Our servers and databases are located in the United States. By using the App, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers from the EEA and UK.
